We won the Todai To Texas Demo Day Award!

2019-09-30

We are proud to announce that HarvestX is awarded the Demo Day Award at Todai to Texas 2020. As a recipient, we will have an exhibition booth at SXSW 2020 to be held in Austin, Texas, USA in March 2020. SXSW(South by Southwest) is held every March in Austin, celebrating the convergence of tech, film, and music industries.

‘Todai To Texas’ is a contest organized by the Division of University Corporate Relations, University of Tokyo. The six winning teams will receive funding for travel expenses and a booth in the interactive section of SXSW.
