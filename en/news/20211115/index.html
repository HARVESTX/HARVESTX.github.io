XV2, Our New Automatic Pollinating and Harvesting Robot

2021-11-15

HarvestX Inc. (Head office: Bunkyo-Ku, Tokyo; President: Yuki Ichikawa) released XV2, a new automatic pollinating and harvesting robot prototype with higher mobility and efficiency than the previous prototype.

New Features of XV2

  • Suitable for multi-layer vertical farming system
  • Autonomous driving by magnetic guidance system
  • Equipped with a function to change harvesting trays to increase loading capacity of harvested strawberries

With this new robot specialized for indoor vertical farms, we will continue to develop products with even greater adaptability to the actual cultivation environment.
