HarvestX partnered with National Institute of Technology, Tokuyama College.

2022-03-30

HarvestX Inc. (Head office: Bunkyo-Ku, Tokyo; President: Yuki Ichikawa) partnered with National Institute of Technology, Tokuyama College and established its second office within the college to start joint research.This is the first time in Japan that a start-up company has established an office in a national institute of technology.

(CEO and CTO of HarvestX and members of Tokuyama College AT Team at Tokuyama College)

Through this collaboration, we can accelerate development of more efficient pollinating attachment toward fully automated agriculture. It also enables us to provide students with an opportunity to access cutting-edge technical information and a place to demonstrate their engineering skills.
