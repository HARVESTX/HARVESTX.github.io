Senior Vice Minister visited HarvestX Lab.

2022-04-19

Hideyuki Tanaka, Senior Vice Minister of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology, visited HarvestX Lab, our research and development facility for a fully automated strawberry cultivation robot system, in the University of Tokyo Entrepreneur Lab. After tasting strawberries grown in the lab, he had discussions with CEO Ichikawa and Tokyo University officials.
