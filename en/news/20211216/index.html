HarvestX Joins Microsoft for Startups

2021-12-16

We are excited to announce that HarvestX has been accepted to the Microsoft for Startups program, which is designed to strengthen the development of promising startups.

Microsoft for Startups helps B2B startups successfully scale their companies by providing them with access to innovative technology, hands-on mentorship, and access to new sales opportunities. The resources and supports provided by Microsoft will allow us to continue to improve our automatic pollinating and harvesting robot.
