We have received a grant from the Mitsubishi UFJ Technology Development Foundation

2021-09-22

We are proud to announce that our robot system development project “Development of Attachments with Dataset Creation Method, Machine Learning and Tool Exchange Mechanism for Pollinating and Harvesting Robots” has received “2021 Research and Development Grants” from the Mitsubishi UFJ Technology Development Foundation. The objective of this grant is to support venture companies in their research and development of new technologies and products.
