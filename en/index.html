ABOUT

HarvestX is an agritech project developing robots to grow and harvest strawberries.

We research and develop robots for plant factories to realize fully automated fruits cultivation to solve the food supply and labor shortage problem in the future. We are now focusing on strawberries and developing robots that support pollination and harvesting that are big burdens for farmers now.

TECHNOLOGY

01

Recognition of Flowers and Fruits

Our robots recognize positions of flowers and fruits with cameras for pollination and picking. They also detect maturity of fruits with machine learning.

02

Special Attachment for Pollination

Our robots pollinate strawberries with a special brush attachment. This technology is patent pending.

03

Harvesting Without Damage

Our robots harvest strawberries by picking stems in order not to damage fruits.
