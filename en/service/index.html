We conduct research and development to establish robotic pollination and harvesting technology and realize fully automated fruit cultivation in plant factories.

Product

Automatic Pollination and Harvesting Demonstrator　XV2

XV2 is our new automatic pollinating and harvesting robot prototype with higher mobility and efficiency than the previous prototype.

New Features of XV2
🍓 Suitable for multi-layer vertical farming system
🍓 Autonomous driving by magnetic guidance system
🍓 Equipped with a function to change harvesting trays to increase loading capacity of harvested strawberries

With this new robot specialized for plant factories, we will continue to develop products with even greater adaptability to the actual cultivation environment.

Automatic Pollination and Harvesting Demonstrator　XV1

XV1 is a demonstration robot for automated strawberry cultivation developed by HarvestX. XV1 uses depth cameras and image processing algorithms designed by HarvestX to recognize flowers and fruit and uses a patent-pending attachment for pollination and harvesting. The robot aims to demonstrate farming operations in a large farmyard with its first electric cart.
