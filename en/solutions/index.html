Indoor Farm Automation
"HarvestX"

Our Solution

In the strawberry production process in indoor farms, we automate "plant management", "pollination", and "harvesting" to achieve stable production.

Advantages

Higher pollination success rate than honey bees, maintaining a sanitary environment in a farm

Robots enable highly accurate pollination. Compared to bees, whose pollination accuracy is affected by even slight environmental changes, the robot has a 27.8% higher pollination accuracy rate and reduces the occurrence of malformed fruit. It also prevents the risk of diseases caused by bee carcasses left unattended and the risk of occupational accidents such as bee stings to workers, thereby creating a hygienic environment.

Automated analysis of seedling condition and yield prediction based on data collected by robots

Robots collect all kinds of data in the indoor vertical farm to automate the analysis of the seedling condition and yield prediction. It is possible to accurately forecast harvest dates and yields with the system managing all individual seedlings. The system also reduces labor costs and detects pests and diseases early by identifying areas that need to be cared for in advance.

Data-driven cultivation and robotic pollination for consistent yield improvement

Based on data obtained by monitoring the environment and growth in the indoor vertical farm, it is possible to control the optimal cultivation environment and predict plant growth. Furthermore, highly accurate automatic pollination technology improves the yield of fruit to sell as products.

Labor-saving and automated harvesting operations significantly reduce production costs

All harvesting operations previously performed by hand are now automated. In addition, the training costs required due to the replacement of workers are also reduced, resulting in a significant reduction in labor costs during the harvesting process. In the future, cultivation support functions will be added sequentially to achieve automated cultivation.

Robot for Indoor Farms "XV3"

XV3 is the core robot of HarvestX, an automation solution for strawberry indoor farms.

High scalability

The XV3 consists of two parts: the XV3 Cart, which automatically drives around the indoor vertical farm, and the XV3 Unit, equipped with sensors for data collection and robotic arms.
The XV3 can accommodate a variety of needs. The height can be freely selected from 1 to 5 tiers according to the number of shelves in the indoor vertical farm. The Unit also can be replaced in response to the work to be performed.
We support future updates of functions, allowing robots in indoor farms to evolve and advancing automation without replacing the hardware on a large scale.
